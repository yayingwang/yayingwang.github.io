Project Summary

Client

Bschool Travel is a travel company co-founded by two current MBA students providing​ "accessible and affordable MBA travel programs", such as pre-MBA treks, cross-MBA trips, country treks, corporate treks as well as customized tours. In addition, they also ​provide​ travel scholarships for students to attend one of the corporate or cross-MBA trips during the summer month.

Initial Proposed Problem

The problem Bschool Travel wanted us to address is how to improv their current working system to engage new customers and to process customers requests to complete the sale more effectively.

Research Process

Background Research

In order to extract valuable information from the interviews and observations, we first each did a background research on the client, the sector, and the problem to gain a deeper understanding of their issues.

I wrote a research report on needs and preferences of their target customers, the MBA and young travelers, and a competitor analysis on how the services of different travel companies are meeting their needs.

user research

Contextual Interviews and Observations

We conducted contextual interviews to different stakeholders, the co-founders, the intern and the customers, to investigate each of their roles, action and pain points in the whole collaboration process.

  • Co-founders
  • Intern
  • Customers (A third party client)

After conducting interviews, we debriefed all the information we learned from each interview and collected more than 300 hundreds affinity notes about their workflow, the ways they communicate with their customers and suppliers, and the pattern of their internal collaboration.

Affinity Diagram

In the next phase, we build an affinity wall (affinity diagram) to gather insights. We clustered related affinity notes in ways enabling us to conclude a thoughtful insight into a higher level note, and repeated the process until we formed a four-levels, structured affinity wall.

Affinity Diagram

Reframing the problem

Based on our analysis, we found that many difficulties Bschool Travel were facing actually steamed from other root problems in their disorganized workflow within the team and disorderly means of communication towards their customers.

I mapped out their work model to indicate their ways of interacting with their customers, suppliers and themselves.

Their working model with customers, suppliers and between themselves.

As a result, we reframed their problems to how to make their overall workflow and collaboration more efficient.

Findings and Recommendations

We concluded issues in three aspects:

  • Communication & Collaboration
  • Roles & Duties
  • Customer Requests & Itineraries Management

As a result, we reframed their problems to how to make their overall workflow and collaboration more efficient.
